Why Us

The main reason people choose us and are happy with our services is this: we make things fun and painless for you.

The process of brainstorming applications and extracting requirements can be challenging. We make it fun for you by taking burden of the difficult parts off your shoulders.

The phase of fine tuning the end result can be tedious. We don't put that on you. Leave the boring stuff to us.

Deploying the app and getting it in the hands of your users is something we have down to a science. Don't sweat it.

Most of all, the software we build integrates seamlessly with your processses, and is delightful to use.

How We Work

By now you know we make things enjoyable for you. Our first step in working together is a meeting with you at your office, at a coffee shop, or wherever else you feel most comfortable. You tell us about your business—your processes, your pain points, your challenges—all the areas where we can create solutions to make your life easier.

From there, we'll come back to you after a few days with ideas and prototypes that may or may not blow your mind. You take these ideas and give your feedback, and we'll come up with a scope of work.

Now the fun begins. We start building the app. We will be in touch with you as often as you like (in the daily to weekly range). You will even be able to see and use app as it's being built, thanks to our secure test environments.

You didn't even realize it because you were having so much fun, but your app is ready. Let's deploy it. Every scenario is different, so let's put the app in the app stores, deploy it to a cloud instance, or put it in your server closet. Whatever the case, we can figure out the logistics so you can sit back, relax, and wait for your app to come to you.
