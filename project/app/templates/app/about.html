About Us

Solving business problems through software, one app at a time

The What

We build web and mobile applications that solve a variety of problems. We help your organization run more efficiently, help generate revenue, attract new customers, and anything else that makes your life easier. From workflow automation, to e-commerce, to startup platforms and everything in between, we do it and we can help.

The How

We are an agile team that embraces modern engineering techniques to build great software. We work quickly yet carefully. We iterate and improve constantly. We do all this in the name of building the applications you need for your organization and will love to use.

The Why

"Windward Apps was born out of need to fix the issue of companies paying too much for subpar software. Over many years working as an employee at various companies, I saw all too often that clients were receiving an end result that was not up to standard—features missing or not implemented correctly, solutions delivered way behind schedule, buggy applications, vendors not respecting clients' budget—the list goes on. I created Windward Apps with one simple principle: bring excellent customer service to the software service industry. With that comes software this is elegant, robust, built to spec, well-engineered, and delivered on time and on budget."
- Kyle Mulleady, Founder
